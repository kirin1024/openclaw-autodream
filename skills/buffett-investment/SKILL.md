## 一、核心理念

### 1. 买股票就是买公司
- **把股票当作生意来看**
- 市场是为你服务的，不是来指导你的
- "If you look at stocks as a business and treat the market as something that doesn't tell you, isn't there to instruct you, but it's there to serve you, you'll do a lot better over time."
- 不要看K线图、均线等技术分析
- "The stock market is a device for transferring money from the impatient to the patient."

### 2. 护城河思维
- 寻找有**持久竞争优势**的公司
- 护城河可以是：品牌、渠道、网络效应、低成本、转换成本等
- "The key is to find businesses with durable competitive advantages."
- 最好的护城河是"消费者行为习惯"（Changing behavior is the hardest thing）

### 3. 安全边际
- 只在价格大幅低于价值时买入
- 好的买入是成功的一半
- "Price is what you pay. Value is what you get."
- "It's far better to buy a wonderful company at a fair price than a fair company at a wonderful price."

### 4. 集中投资
- 芒格："一生只需要做对几个决定"
- "Charlie always thought I did too many things. He about five things in our lifetime, we thought if we did would end up doing better than if we did 50."
- 普通人持有3-5只股票就够了
- "You don't need to own 50 stocks. It's a very serious mistake."

### 5. 长期持有
- 最好的公司值得终身持有
- "Our favorite holding period is forever."
- 频繁交易会错失复利效应
- "Someone's sitting in the shade today because someone planted a tree a long time ago."

## 二、投资方法论

### 如何选股

| 维度 | 巴菲特的检查清单 |
|------|------------------|
| **商业模式** | 简单易懂？长期稳定？ |
| **护城河** | 竞争对手难以复制？ |
| **管理层** | 诚实、能干、为股东考虑？ |
| **财务指标** | 高ROE、稳定盈利、自由现金流？ |
| **估值** | 内在价值显著低于价格？ |

### 财务报表优先看什么？
- **资产负债表 > 利润表**
- "Focus on balance sheets over income statements"
- 现金为王
- 负债要健康
- "The best business is one that earns a lot of money on no incremental capital"

### 不做什么？
- 不做空
- 不做不懂的事
- 不买贵的股票
- 不理会宏观经济预测
- "Nobody knows what the market is going to do tomorrow, next week, or next month. But they spend all their time talking about it because it's easy to talk about, though it has no value."
- 避免使用杠杆

## 三、市场观点

### 关于择时
- 等待"好球"（fat pitch）
- "Things get extraordinarily attractive very occasionally."
- "We have three options: to buy small pieces of businesses through the stock market, to buy whole businesses, or to do nothing. Usually we do nothing."
- 现金不是垃圾，等待机会

### 关于恐慌
- "Be fearful when others are greedy, and greedy when others are fearful"
- 大跌时往往是机会
- 别人恐慌时你要贪婪
- "When it's raining gold, put out your bucket"

### 关于泡沫
- 不要跟随大众
- "Don't get talked out of doing what has worked in the past, don't get carried away with fads"
- 避免追逐热点
- "The most important thing to do if you find yourself in a hole is to stop digging"

## 四、投资检查清单（买入前必问）

### 必问7条
- [ ] 这家公司商业模式简单易懂吗？
- [ ] 它有什么护城河？
- [ ] 管理层诚实且有能力吗？
- [ ] 过去10年ROE > 15%吗？
- [ ] 有稳定的自由现金流吗？
- [ ] 估值有安全边际吗？
- [ ] 能持有10年吗？

### 卖出信号
- [ ] 基本面变坏
- [ ] 估值过高
- [ ] 发现更好的机会
- [ ] 需要现金
